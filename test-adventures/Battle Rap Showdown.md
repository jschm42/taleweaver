Here is the concept for your Text-Adventure scene, optimized for a high-stakes Battle-Rap showdown.

## ---

**The Scene: "The Cauldron of Bars"**

The air is thick with the smell of cheap beer and anticipation. You are standing on a makeshift stage built from shipping pallets. In front of you is a sea of judgmental faces—the **Crowd**. Opposite you stands **"Iron Tongue,"** the reigning king of the city's underground scene.

### **The Core Mechanic: The Hype Meter**

The game tracks a single variable: **Crowd Favor** ($CF$).

* **$CF \= 0$**: The crowd is indifferent or biased toward Iron Tongue.  
* **$CF \= 100$**: Total dominance. The crowd screams your name; Iron Tongue is silenced.  
* **$CF \= \-100$**: You are booed off stage. Career over.

### ---

**NPCs**

* **Iron Tongue:** The antagonist. He is fast, aggressive, and uses "Personal Attacks" to rattle you.  
* **The Crowd:** An unpredictable entity. They value three things: **Flow**, **Wit**, and **Fearlessness**.  
* **DJ Scratch:** Provides the beat. You can signal him to change the tempo to throw Iron Tongue off-balance.

### ---

**The Gameplay: "The Lyrical Duel"**

Instead of traditional combat, you use **Verbal Maneuvers**. Every turn, Iron Tongue attacks, and you must choose a response based on the crowd's current mood.

#### **Command Options:**

1. **Rebuttal**: Use Iron Tongue’s last rhyme against him.  
   * *Effect:* High $CF$ gain if successful.  
   * *Requirement:* You must have "Listened" (skipped a minor boost) in the previous turn.  
2. **Multisyllabic Scheme**: Deliver a complex rhythmic pattern.  
   * *Effect:* Gains respect from the "Hip-Hop Heads" in the front row.  
3. **Self-Deprecation**: Make fun of yourself before he can.  
   * *Effect:* Neutralizes his next "Personal Attack" and makes the crowd find you relatable.  
4. **Crowd Participation**: Point the mic at the audience.  
   * *Effect:* If $CF \> 50$, they roar, doubling your next turn's score. If $CF \< 50$, they stay silent (massive $CF$ loss).

### ---

**Items (Equipped via Backstory)**

* **The "Rough Draft" Note:** A crumpled piece of paper. Use it to "Recall" a pre-written punchline (Guaranteed hit, one-time use).  
* **Throat Spray:** Prevents "Choking" (a random debuff that happens when the crowd boos too loudly).

### ---

**Sample Interaction**

**Iron Tongue:** *"You look like a tourist, lost in the slums / I’m the king of the rhythm, you’re just picking up crumbs\!"*

**The Crowd:** *"Oohhhh\!"* (Hype Meter shifts toward Iron Tongue).

**Action Menu:**

1. Rebuttal: "I’m a tourist? Then your girl is my guide."  
2. Flow: \[Technical Fast Rap about his repetitive rhymes\]  
3. Stage Move: \[Drop the mic to your left hand and stare him down\]

### ---

**Victory & Failure**

* **Victory:** The crowd begins a "One more bar\!" chant. Iron Tongue lowers his head and hands you the "Golden Mic." You have won the city.  
* **Failure:** The "Boo" reaches a deafening volume. The DJ cuts the beat. The screen fades to black with the text: *“Don’t quit your day job.”*
